About Jemma

Jemma is a 7-year-old female cat who has been my companion since she was a kitten. She has a strong personality but is truly a love bug at heart. Recently, I noticed her constantly licking her lips and rubbing her mouth, which led me to take her to the vet.

Unfortunately, she is in urgent need of dental surgery, and I cannot afford it alone. The vet has estimated the total cost to be $2,316, covering extractions, medication, and follow-up care. My goal is to relieve her pain and ensure she continues living a happy, healthy life.
