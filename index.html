About Jemma

Jemma is a 7-year-old female cat who has been my companion since she was a kitten. She has a strong personality but is truly a love bug at heart. She was in need of dental surgery due to dental disease.

Updates

She had dental surgery on March 26th at 8 AM and came home at 4 PM the same day. Jemma had six teeth removed and her gums cleaned. This was all possible because of you! Thank you for every single donation!

She is currently on two medications for pain management and resting in the gaming room. She has to eat wet food for a month and be isolated for four days due to a long-lasting medication. Below are some photos of her dental procedure and X-rays.

Again, thank you! You are truly kind people, and I appreciate you all!
